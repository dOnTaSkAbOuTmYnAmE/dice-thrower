How does this work?

A few dozen gnomes throw given dices and write down results, then give them to math wizards that then with some magic creates the graph you see

But all joking aside that is pretty much how it works. First numbers between 1 & <Dice> are generated <Number of dice> times and summed up. Then it is repeated <Number of throws> times, the data is saved and displayed
